About Innovative Upshifts LLC

We support learners and families during key transitions, elementary to middle school, high school to college or career, and changes in support plans.

Our approach blends evidence-based practices with compassionate advocacy, ensuring each student has a clear plan and the tools to thrive.

Our Values

  • Dignity and respect
  • Strengths-based planning
  • Collaboration and communication
  • Measurable progress

Who We Serve

Students grades 4-12, young adults, and families seeking support navigating IEPs/504s, executive function challenges, college readiness, and community services.

Founder

With over a decade of specialized experience at Hill-Freedman World Academy, my mission is to empower students with unique learning needs. As a National Board Certified educator, I have honed skills in special education and developed a keen expertise in transition services, autism spectrum disorders, and other learning needs, ensuring that each student's educational journey is as unique as they are.

My role encompasses more than instruction; it's about fostering independence and preparing students for a productive future. By leveraging assistive technology and creating tailored behavior plans, our team has cultivated a learning environment where students with diverse abilities can thrive and transition confidently into their next phase of life.
